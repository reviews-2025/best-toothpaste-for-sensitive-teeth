Why Are Your Teeth So Sensitive?

Tooth sensitivity usually occurs when the protective enamel on your teeth wears down, exposing the softer dentin underneath. This can happen due to several reasons, such as:

  • Brushing too hard
  • Consuming acidic foods and drinks
  • Grinding your teeth at night
  • Using whitening products excessively

The Problem with Commercial Toothpastes

Many over-the-counter toothpastes claim to help with sensitivity, but some contain harsh chemicals that can worsen the problem over time. They often mask the symptoms instead of addressing the root cause. This is why exploring natural solutions can be a game-changer.

How to Make the Best Toothpaste for Sensitive Teeth at Home

This homemade toothpaste uses natural ingredients that not only reduce sensitivity but also improve overall oral health. Here’s what you’ll need:

  • 1 tablespoon of coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 2 drops of clove essential oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon of turmeric powder

Simply mix all the ingredients in a small bowl until you achieve a paste-like consistency. Store it in a clean, airtight container, and use it as you would regular toothpaste. You’ll notice a difference in just a few uses!
